## 5. Decision Log

1. I classify MediWound as Pharma & Therapeutics rather than Biotechnology because the decisive evidence is not just platform biology but regulated drug development and marketed biologic therapeutics with FDA and EMA approvals. [S2][S5][S6][S9][S10][S11]
2. I assign Trauma-Tech because the approved flagship product is for severe thermal burns and eschar removal in acute injury settings. [S2][S5][S6][S9][S10]
3. I assign Dermatology as the primary clinical domain because the actual treated tissue and indications are skin burns and skin-wound debridement. [S2][S3][S5][S6][S11]
4. I keep Surgery as a secondary clinical domain only because the core clinical claim is reduction of surgical debridement and graft burden, not a surgical instrument or workflow business. [S6][S7][S8]
5. I mark legal registry fields as N/A because I did not independently retrieve Israeli registrar records in this pass. [S4]
