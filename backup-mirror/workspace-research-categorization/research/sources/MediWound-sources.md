# MediWound Source Notes

Research date: 2026-06-03.

## 1. Company Materials

- [S1] https://mediwound.com/
  Key extract: MediWound describes itself as a biotechnology company focused on next-generation enzymatic therapeutics for non-surgical tissue repair.

- [S2] https://mediwound.com/product/nexobrid/
  Key extract: NexoBrid is a topically administered biological orphan drug for enzymatic removal of eschar in deep partial- and full-thickness thermal burns and is approved in the U.S., EU, Japan, and other markets.

- [S3] https://mediwound.com/product/escharex/
  Key extract: EscharEx is a bromelain-based enzymatic therapy in advanced clinical development for chronic and hard-to-heal wounds, with a global Phase III study in venous leg ulcers underway.

## 3. Clinical Literature

- [S7] https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37455553/
  Key extract: Systematic review concludes NexoBrid is a safe, selective, non-surgical eschar-removal modality associated with faster debridement and fewer operations.
